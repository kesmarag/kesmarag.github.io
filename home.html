Short Bio

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Statistics and Actuarial - Financial Mathematics at the University of the Aegean. I earned a PhD in Applied Mathematics from the University of Crete in 2019. After completing my PhD, I was a post-doctoral researcher at the School of Applied Mathematical and Physical Sciences in the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), at the Institute of Applied and Computational Mathematics of the Foundation for Research and Technology - Hellas (FORTH), as well as at the Institute of Geodynamics in the National Observatory of Athens (NOA). In addition, I held the position of an Adjunct Lecturer at the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics in the University of Crete (UoC).

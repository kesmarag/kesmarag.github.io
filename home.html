Short Bio

I am an Assistant Professor in Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing at the Department of Statistics and Actuarial - Financial Mathematics at the University of the Aegean. I am also a Member of the Institute of Applied and Computational Mathematics (IACM) of FORTH.

Past Teaching

Over the years, I have had the privilege of teaching a diverse range of courses. My teaching experience spans both foundational and advanced subjects in mathematics, including Analytical Geometry and Complex Numbers, Applied Linear Algebra I & II, Differential and Partial Differential Equations, Numerical Analysis, Calculus II, Mathematical Finance, Wave Propagation, as well as statistics courses such as Descriptive, Parametric, and Applied Statistics.

Fun fact: Since the Fall 2020 semester, I have taught at least one new course each teaching semester, a challenge I embrace as it allows me to continually broaden my academic teaching experience.
